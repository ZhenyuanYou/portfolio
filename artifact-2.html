Artifact 02 · Strategic timeline

The Evolution of AI

This artifact reframes a timeline project as a visual strategic narrative. Instead of hiding the work behind a download button, the key AI milestones are visible and easy to scan on-page.

What this artifact demonstrates

Strategic communication through visual storytelling

This artifact translates technical history into a structure that non-technical stakeholders can understand quickly. It shows my ability to organize complex information into accessible, decision-oriented communication.

Audience adaptation

Built for instructors, recruiters, and managers

The page highlights major shifts in AI through visible milestones, short commentary, and a clear strategic takeaway rather than long academic paragraphs.

Visual Timeline

A simplified on-page version of the historical sequence that drives the broader strategic analysis.

1950s–1960s · Symbolic AI Rule-based systems and symbolic reasoning dominated early AI thinking, focusing on logic, explicit rules, and human-designed knowledge representation.
1980s–1990s · Statistical Machine Learning AI momentum shifted toward probabilistic and data-driven techniques, enabling systems to learn patterns from structured data rather than relying only on hard-coded rules.
2010s · Deep Learning Increased compute power, large datasets, and neural network breakthroughs made end-to-end representation learning practical for image, speech, and sequence tasks.
2020s · Generative AI Transformer architectures, scaled pretraining, and interface design converged to make AI broadly accessible and commercially significant across industries.
Driver 1

Data

Large datasets enabled models to move from hand-engineered intelligence toward scalable pattern recognition and generation.

Driver 2

Compute

GPU acceleration and distributed systems made deep learning and large-scale training economically feasible.

Driver 3

Architecture

Transformers redefined the design space for language, multimodal systems, and foundation model deployment.

Strategic takeaway: this artifact is not only about AI history. It shows how technological progress becomes useful when it is framed clearly for business, educational, and decision-making audiences.
