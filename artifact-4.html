Artifact 04 · Safe Continuous Learning System

A system-level design for safely retraining and deploying machine learning models in production environments, with a focus on reliability, monitoring, and rollback strategies.

Data & Training

Collect new episodes, filter noisy data, and train candidate models offline using versioned datasets and reproducible pipelines.

Validation & Safety

Evaluate models on fixed and recent datasets, and test in simulation environments to ensure stability and safety before deployment.

Deployment & Monitoring

Use canary deployment and real-time monitoring. Automatically rollback models when performance drops or safety risks are detected.

Promote Model

  • Validation ≥ baseline
  • Recent performance improved
  • No safety violations
  • Stable metrics

Rollback Model

  • Success rate < 88%
  • ≥3% performance drop
  • Safety interrupt triggered
  • Unexpected behavior
