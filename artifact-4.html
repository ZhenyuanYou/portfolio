Artifact 04 · MLOps design

Safe Continuous Learning System

A production-level design for safely retraining, validating, deploying, monitoring, and rolling back machine learning models in dynamic environments. This artifact demonstrates system thinking rather than model training alone.

End-to-End Pipeline

The design below emphasizes staged updates, safety review, and clear rollback conditions instead of risky unreviewed online learning.

Step 1Collect DataLog new episodes and outcomes.
Step 2Filter & CleanRemove corrupt, duplicated, or unreliable samples.
Step 3Train OfflineBuild a versioned candidate model.
Step 4ValidateTest on fixed and recent datasets plus simulation.
Step 5Canary DeployRoll out to a small monitored subset.
Step 6Monitor & RollbackRevert automatically if risk appears.

Key Evaluation Metrics

The promotion decision depends on more than accuracy. Safety, stability, and recent-environment performance matter as much as general validation.

90%+Target success rate
< 2.5%False-pick tolerance
0Critical safety violations

Deployment Decision Logic

This artifact is designed to be easy to scan: one side shows when the candidate model can move forward; the other shows when it must be rolled back.

Promote model

Conditions to move forward

  • Validation performance is at or above baseline.
  • Recent data performance improves on the current environment.
  • No critical safety violations appear in simulation or canary.
  • Metrics remain stable enough for staged expansion.
Rollback model

Conditions to revert immediately

  • Success rate drops below the defined floor.
  • Relative regression exceeds the allowed threshold.
  • Safety interrupt or physical safety trip occurs.
  • Unexpected behavior or reward-hacking pattern appears.
Simulation timeline

Example cycle

Day 0 Drift is detected and retraining is triggered.
Day 1 A new data snapshot is created and recent holdout data is reserved.
Day 2 Candidate model training and offline evaluation are completed.
Day 3 Canary rollout begins with close monitoring.
Day 4 If a safety event occurs, rollback happens immediately.
Day 5 Root cause is isolated, fixed, and the cycle restarts safely.
